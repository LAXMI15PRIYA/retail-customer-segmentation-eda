# Retail Customer Segmentation – EDA

Exploratory data analysis on a retail customer dataset to understand what drives 
monthly revenue and uncover patterns useful for customer segmentation.

## Dataset
200 customers, 9 features:
- `CustomerID`, `Age`, `Gender`, `CityTier`, `MonthlyIncome`, `TenureMonths`, 
  `VisitsPerMonth`, `AvgOrderValue`, `MonthlyRevenue`

## Analysis Performed
**Univariate Analysis**
- Average monthly revenue per customer
- Age distribution across the customer base
- Monthly income distribution

**Bivariate Analysis**
- Average revenue by city tier
- Revenue comparison: male vs. female customers
- Correlation between visit frequency and revenue
